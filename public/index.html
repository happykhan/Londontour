      About Londontour

      Londontour is a self-hosted London walking map with guided routes, browse layers, search, nearby exploration, and offline download support.

      Data and licences

      • Basemap tiles are self-hosted PMTiles generated from OpenStreetMap data. Map data is © OpenStreetMap contributors and licensed under the Open Database Licence.
      • Commercial use of OpenStreetMap data is allowed with attribution and ODbL compliance.
      • Map rendering uses MapLibre GL JS, licensed BSD-3-Clause.
      • Single-file tile loading uses PMTiles. PMTiles reference implementations are BSD-3-Clause; the PMTiles specification is public domain/CC0 where applicable.
      • Basemap styling uses Protomaps/OpenMapTiles-style vector data. Protomaps basemap style assets are CC0, while OpenStreetMap-derived map data remains ODbL.
      • Transport overlays use TfL Open Data and OpenStreetMap-derived geometry where available. Live services, stops, pier access, and route changes should be checked with TfL or the relevant operator.
      • POI layers are generated from OpenStreetMap and public place metadata. Opening times, access, websites, and facilities can change.

      This app is informational and comes with no guarantee of route availability, opening hours, accessibility, or transport service status.
